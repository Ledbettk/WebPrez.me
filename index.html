The Workshop

WebPrez.me is a student-run workshop at the University of Colorado Boulder dedicated to teaching fellow students the basics of building a website, expanding their web presence, and adding valuable skills to their resume. Designed for students with beginner to intermediate tech skills, this free Web Dev workshop guartenees to get each student up and running with their own personal site by the end of the semester.

Build Your Web Presence

We live in a fast paced, tech-dominated society in which most of our online activity is available for others to see. Have something aside from Twitter and Facebook that represents who you are. Stay ahead of the game and learn the tech-skills employers are looking for

Expand From Your Resume

Building a website gives you the opportunity to share additional information about yourself that a resume doesn't reflect. Writing samples, photography, that super impressive project you made in that one class -- get creative and show a different side of yourself

Tell Your Story

You are your brand, and every brand has a story. Every student has something that makes them unique, and having a website allows you to share your story with the world and set yourself apart from the masses. Get creative and build something you can be proud of

Utility

We, as Millennials, are a hot commodity for any company. We bring a fresh perspective on life and are more adaptive to new technologies. By having a custom website, you are able to showcase your skills, hobbies, and experience beyond your resume.

Get that dream job/internship

Demonstrate to employers that you are a tech-savvy individual who can add value to their company

Make something you can be proud of

making a custom website is no easy job, but after this workshop you will have a site to show off to friends and family, and a new set of skills to build on
